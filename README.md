# Unguentarii-and-Rome-Perfums
The present work offers an overview of the world of perfumes and unguents in Early Empire period. In order to do this, we fi rst analyzed the methods and techniques used for the production of unguents and, second, the role played by the artisans dedicated to the perfume trade in Rome, the unguentarii. We emphasize these characters fi rst, as individuals, where we talk about their social status, marital status, gender, age, origin, etc., secondly, as belonging members of some familia or some gens, third, by the place where they performed their work and lastly, during that period of time they performed this profession
